How to get started
01
Install
Run the installer, click through the wizard. It drops the plugin into your VST3 folder automatically.
02
Add to a track
In your DAW, add MicInput as an instrument on any track. Select your microphone from the dropdown at the top.
03
Turn on Auto-save
Hit the Auto-save toggle in the Takes tab. Now every time you hit record in your DAW, your mic is captured automatically.
04
Find your best take
Browse takes in the plugin, run transcription to read what you recorded, then drag the keeper straight to your DAW timeline.
